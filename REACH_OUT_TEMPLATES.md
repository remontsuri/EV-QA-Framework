Лучший способ — это **личный контакт** через LinkedIn с инженерами или рекрутерами. Холодные письма работают лучше, чем просто отклик на вакансию.

Вот 3 готовых варианта сообщения. Выберите тот, который подходит к ситуации.

### 🥈 Вариант 2: Сопроводительное письмо (Cover Letter)
**Куда вставлять:** В поле "Cover Letter" при подаче заявки на сайте Tesla Careers.

**Текст:**
> Dear Hiring Team,
> 
> Quality assurance in Electric Vehicles is not just about passing tests; it's about predicting failures before they happen. That is the philosophy behind my open-source project, **EV-QA-Framework**, which I am excited to submit as part of my application.
> 
> Recognizing the critical importance of Battery Management System (BMS) data integrity, I engineered a Python framework that goes beyond simple threshold checks. Key technical achievements include:
> 
> 1.  **Machine Learning Integration**: Implemented an Isolation Forest algorithm to detect non-linear anomalies in telemetry streams (voltage, current, temperature).
> 2.  **Robust Architecture**: Utilized Pydantic models for strict data validation, ensuring type safety across the pipeline.
> 3.  **Production-Ready Quality**: Achieved ~90% test coverage with over 85 automated tests, integrated into a GitHub Actions CI/CD pipeline.
> 
> You can review the code and documentation here: https://github.com/remontsuri/EV-QA-Framework
> 
> My practical experience building test automation tools from scratch allows me to immediately contribute to Tesla’s mission of accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy. I look forward to discussing how I can help your QA team scale.
> 
> Sincerely,
> [Your Name]
